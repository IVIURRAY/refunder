# RefundAgent — Product Overview

> A non-technical overview of what RefundAgent does, who it is for, and where it is going. Written for use as the basis of a sign-up landing page.

---

## The Problem

Every year, UK and EU consumers are owed billions of pounds in refunds and compensation they never claim. Not because they don't know they're entitled — but because claiming is a grind.

A delayed flight means logging into an airline portal, finding the right form, calculating your compensation tier, citing the right regulation, and then chasing for weeks when they ignore you. A faulty return means tracking down an order number, writing a formal complaint, and knowing whether to quote the Consumer Rights Act or just threaten a chargeback. A disputed car rental charge means digging out pickup photos you may not have taken.

Most people don't bother. The companies that owe them money are counting on that.

---

## What RefundAgent Does

**RefundAgent is an AI assistant that watches your inbox and handles your refund claims for you.**

You forward a refund-related email — a delayed flight notification, a rejected return, a rental dispute — to a single address. RefundAgent reads it, understands what you're owed, and takes it from there.

No forms. No chasing. No needing to know your rights.

---

## Today — What the MVP Delivers

The current version focuses on one thing: **making sure no refund opportunity slips through the cracks**.

When an email arrives, RefundAgent automatically:

- **Identifies** whether it relates to a refund or compensation claim
- **Extracts** the key details — merchant, amount, reference number, flight details, dates
- **Categorises** the claim by type (flight delay, retail return, car rental dispute)
- **Tracks** the status as replies come in — pending, acknowledged, resolved, rejected
- **Alerts you** when a claim needs your attention

You get a clear, up-to-date picture of every open claim — and a record of everything that's been exchanged — without having to manage it yourself.

**What RefundAgent covers today:**

| Claim type | What it tracks |
|---|---|
| ✈ Flight delays & cancellations | EU261/UK261 compensation (up to £520 per passenger) |
| 🛍 Retail & e-commerce | Refunds, faulty goods, cooling-off period returns |
| 🚗 Car rental disputes | Damage charges, deposit delays, fuel penalties |

---

## Coming Next — Automated Claim Filing

The next version closes the loop: rather than just tracking what you're owed, RefundAgent **files the claim on your behalf**.

When it detects a valid compensation opportunity, it will:

- Draft a formal claim letter citing the right legislation and calculated amount
- Send it from your own email address — so replies come back to you
- Monitor for a response and classify it automatically
- Send a follow-up chase if the merchant goes quiet
- Escalate the tone progressively until the claim is resolved

The goal is a system that requires your attention only when a decision genuinely needs you — accepting a settlement offer, for example, or deciding whether to take a claim to court.

---

## The Longer-Term Vision

**RefundAgent is building towards a fully autonomous claims agent.**

Beyond email, merchants increasingly require claims to be submitted through their own online portals and forms. The roadmap includes:

- **Form automation** — RefundAgent navigates to the merchant's website and submits the claim form directly, using AI to handle different form layouts across hundreds of merchants
- **Document handling** — boarding passes, receipts, and rental agreements are read and used to populate claim evidence automatically
- **Escalation management** — when direct claims fail, RefundAgent routes to alternative dispute resolution (CEDR, BVRLA) or prepares a small claims court filing

The end state is simple: you receive money that was already owed to you, without spending a minute of your own time claiming it.

## Why RefundAgent

**It knows your rights so you don't have to.**
Consumer protection law is complex and varies by claim type. RefundAgent understands the regulations — EU261, the Consumer Rights Act, Section 75 — and applies them correctly, every time.

**It's proactive, not reactive.**
Most people only notice a refund opportunity when they think to look. RefundAgent works continuously in the background, so nothing is missed just because life got busy.

**You stay in control.**
Every claim is visible, every email is logged, and every action requires your approval before it becomes irreversible. RefundAgent acts as your agent — not in place of you.

**Your inbox, your identity.**
Claims are sent from your own email address, not a third-party service. Merchants respond directly to you. There's no intermediary layer that could slow things down or obscure what's happening.

---

## Who It's For

RefundAgent is designed for anyone who regularly books flights, shops online, or rents cars — and who finds the process of chasing refunds and compensation more effort than it's worth.

Early access is focused on individual users in the UK. The service will expand to support multiple users and households, and eventually to the broader EU market.
